Skatelligence

Skatelligence is an AI powered high-performance training tool for figure skaters. Using IMUs mounted to the skater's body, Skatelligence is able to detect and categorize the jumps they perform, and provide valuable insights into their performance.

Keyflow Assist

Individuals with cerebral palsy (CP) struggle to control their hands and arms for precise movements. Playing the piano as physical therapy can help improve these skills. However, weak upper body muscles in CP patients make it difficult. To solve this issue, we designed a device to make piano-focused physical therapy possible for people with CP.

Woodpecker Toy

The woodpecker toy is a 3D printed mechanical toy featuring a woodpecker that pecks on a tree with a push of a button. It designed this project with SolidWorks.
